ABOUT US

Welcome to Parajuli Phone Repair Services, your trusted partner for all your phone repair needs. Our mission is to provide reliable, efficient, and affordable repair services that keep you connected to the things that matter most.

Our Story

Parajuli Phone Repair Services began with a simple idea: to offer high-quality repair services that everyone can trust. Our founders, tech enthusiasts with years of experience in the electronics industry, noticed a gap in the market for honest and dependable phone repair services. Determined to fill this gap, they established Parajuli Phone Repair Services with a focus on transparency, expertise, and exceptional customer service.

What We Do

We specialise in repairing a wide range of phone issues, including cracked screens, battery replacements, and more. Our technicians use state-of-the-art equipment and genuine parts to ensure that your device is restored to its original condition. We service all major brands, including Apple, Samsung, Google, and more.

Why Choose Us?

Expert Technicians: Our skilled technicians are trained to handle the latest phone models and repair techniques, ensuring your device is in capable hands.

Fast Turnaround: We understand the importance of your phone in your daily life, which is why we offer quick repairs for most issues.

Quality Parts: We use only high-quality, genuine parts to guarantee the longevity and performance of your device.

Customer Satisfaction: Our top priority is your satisfaction. We offer a warranty on all our repairs to give you peace of mind.

Transparent Pricing: No hidden fees or surprises. We provide clear, upfront pricing so you know exactly what to expect.

Our Values

At Parajuli Phone Repair Services, we are guided by a set of core values that shape everything we do:

Integrity: We believe in honest and transparent service. You can trust us to provide accurate diagnoses and fair pricing.

Quality: We strive for excellence in every repair, ensuring that each device we fix meets the highest standards of quality.

Customer-Centric: Your needs are at the forefront of our business. We listen to your concerns and tailor our services to meet your specific requirements.

Innovation: Technology is constantly evolving, and so are we. We stay updated with the latest industry trends and repair techniques to provide the best possible service.
